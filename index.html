I am a theorist doing my Ph.D. under Xiao-Gang Wen and have a wide range of interests pertaining to emergent phenomena in quantum matter and field theory. My primary interest at the moment is generalized symmetries and 't Hooft anomalies, including their applications to classifying phases of matter, characterizing phase transitions, and symmetric tensor gauge theories. I am also interested in topological order, boundaries of topologically ordered phases, quantum algebra/topology, emergent gravity in condensed matter, quantum spin liquids, and topologically protected defects in ordered media.
